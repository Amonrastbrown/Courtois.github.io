Early Life of Thibaut Courtois

Thibaut Nicolas Marc Courtois was born on May 11, 1992, in Bree, Belgium.

He grew up in a sports-oriented family—both of his parents,
Thierry Courtois and Gitte Courtois, were volleyball players, and his sister, Valérie Courtois, also became a professional volleyball player for Belgium's national team.

Courtois began playing football at a very young age, originally as a left-back for a local club called Bilzen V.V.

However, when he joined Genk's youth academy at age 7, coaches noticed his potential as a goalkeeper and moved him to that position.


His talent developed quickly, and by age 16, he made his professional debut for K.R.C. Genk in the Belgian Pro League.
