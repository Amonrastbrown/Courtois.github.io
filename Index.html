Who is Thibaut Courtois?

Thibaut Courtois is a Belgian professional goalkeeper who currently plays for Real Madrid.
He is widely regarded as one of the best goalkeepers in the world,
known for his excellent shot-stopping ability, reflexes, and commanding presence in the box.
